Project Thor

started in June 2017 ongoing

rameder. Anhaengerkupplungen und Autoteile GmbH
Retailer for car parts, Germany

Automatization of Reporting Tools

The main part of the job was about the automatization of an already existing reporting tool for daily management reports. Our service was to analyze the current process of how the reports were produced, to locate efficiency lags and to automatize the efficiently structured process of generating the reports via Microft Excel and VBA programmatics. The project is ongoing in that we currently execute these reports daily and work on the further development of an even more efficient process.

Project Iron Man

August 2016

Concardis GmbH
Payment Service Provider, Germany

Design and Developping of Campaign Management

This was a smaller project, where the job was to create a tool via Microsoft Excel and VBA programmatics, to manage customer data for marketing campaigns. We developped a tool to be used for analyzing an input of customer data (high scales, 10'000 and more), selecting specific groups of customers, and creating 0- and Test-groups to test the success of different marketing campaigns. The project was successfully accomplished after 2 weeks.
